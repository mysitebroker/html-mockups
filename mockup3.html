Our Story

Crafting Confidence Since 2010

THRPY Suites was founded with a simple mission: to create a space where the art of traditional barbering meets modern luxury. Our master barbers combine time-honored techniques with contemporary styles to deliver an unparalleled grooming experience.

Every detail of our space has been thoughtfully designed to provide comfort, relaxation, and exceptional service. From our premium leather chairs to our carefully curated product selection, we've created an environment that celebrates the gentleman's grooming tradition.
